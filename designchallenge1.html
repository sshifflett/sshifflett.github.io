Which two websites are you most inspired by?

I was insired by BrightByte Studio and We Are Cuppa.

How do they exhibit effective use of space, size or balance?

Both similar in their use of a hero image to learn more about their work, with similar visual hierarchy. They both have a great balance in their use of space and size.

What makes them similiar and different?

Personally, I prefer the lightness of We Are Cuppa, but prefer Brightbyte Studio’s segmentation of the one page scroll. I like when the navigation goes with you as you scroll down the page, like it does on the BrightByte Studio’s does, but I believe the compactness of the other site is ok without it.
